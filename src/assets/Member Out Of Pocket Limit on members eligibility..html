Member Out Of Pocket Limit | The Out of Pocket provides a standard calculation methods to provide an estimate of what consumers may expect to pay out of pocket for a set of services based on members eligibility.

1.0.0
(0 reviews)Rate and review
Add description

This API provides an opinionated view of members, membership and coverage according to the enterprise across plans, covered states and lines of business.

Returns member and membership information including memberId

Includes the following endpoints:

  • Member Search /v2/members/search
  • Member /v2/members/{memberId}
  • Coverage /v2/members/{memberId}/coverages
  • Includes the following output:
  • member information (in member and member search endpoints)
  • membership information (in member and member search endpoints)
  • brief coverage information (in member and member search endpoints)
  • memberId (used to call other endpoints such as coverage, spending accounts, etc)
  • coverage information (in coverage endpoint)

URL

  • GET /member/{memberId} HTTP/1.1
  • Host: https://anypoint.mulesoft.com:443
  • username: **
  • password: **

Request Payload
- This is a GET operation. Hence no need to pass any payload
